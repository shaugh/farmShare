Plant a seed, watch it grow

Farm Share offers you the unique opportunity to become a share farmer, partnering with a farming family to grow and produce much-needed agricultural resources in Australia.

The farming community is vital to the future of Australians: they keep us fed, clothed, and contribute greatly to the benefits Australians enjoy from exporting from our bread basket.

More words about heaps of cool things that happen, and start to break down the offering from Farm Share. Here is where we spell out the access to updates, learning blogs/videos, access to the farm, small gifts like a bag of seed etc. This should be really easy to read and absorb quickly.

Keep Updated

Our farmers love to share their story and will provide regular updates on how you crop is faring, as well as keep you informed of the challenges/opportunities that are present in every day farming.

See your investment grow

You can closely follow your crop from sowing to reaping, with the option to share your progress with social media, or download as an album (great for school projects!).

Give the gift of growth

Farm Share can be purchased as a gift for a friend or loved one! A totally unique Experience that provides tangible benefits to the community and the share-farmer. Yee-haw!

This is only the beginning

As Farm Share continues to grow, we are hoping to open up new areas of Australian agriculture for you and your friends to experience. Fancy following the life of your very own heifer? Interested in watching a lamb grow to a hogget and get a sample of the wool that it produces? Learn from the source of truth which practices are used and why a farmer must make the decisions that shape the output of their plot.
