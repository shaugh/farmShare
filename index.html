Plant a seed, watch it grow

Farm Share offers you the unique opportunity to become a share farmer, partnering with a farming family in rural Victoria to grow and produce much-needed agricultural resources in rural Victoria. Forget about Farming games, this is real life, and you can be involved!

The farming community is vital to the future of Australians: they keep us fed, clothed, and contribute greatly to the benefits Australians enjoy from exporting from our bread basket.

Keep up to date with the progress of the farm, whats happening now to impact the future, and why. How about access to blogs and videos about farming and the trials and tribulations of everyday farming? As well as all this great stuff, you could also turn a profit and give yourself the choice to cash out, invest in more opportunities or donate a portion to a charity supporting Australian Farmers. You'll have never followed the weather so keenly, as when your invested in it!

Keep Updated

Our farmers love to share their story and will provide regular updates on how your crop is faring, as well as keep you informed of the challenges/opportunities that are present in every day farming. Ride the highs and lows, whilst learning about techniques and practices that are used to achieve prosperous yields.

See your investment grow

You can closely follow your crop from sowing through to harvest, with all the detail along the way. Share the progress via Social Media, or create an album (great for school projects!). Giving you the option to cash out, roll your share over into a new season, or donate to a charity leaves you in charge of who benefits.

Give the gift of growth

Farm Share can be purchased as a gift for a friend or loved one! A totally unique Experience that provides insight into the farming world Australia was built on. Yee-haw! Until now unless you knew someone on a farm, you were not able to experience what its like being a Primary Producer in Australia.

This is only the beginning

As Farm Share continues to grow, we are hoping to open up new areas of Australian Primary Production for you and your friends to experience. Fancy following the life of your very own heifer? Or watch a lamb grow to a hogget and sample its wool? Learn from the source of truth which practices are used and why a farmer must make the decisions they do. What about Farm open days & Live Streams? The world is our oyster.
